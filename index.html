The Hangman

How to play

You guess a word, computer tries to solve it.

  1. Guess a secret word
  2. Enter it's length on top of the page
  3. Computer will guess a letter
  4. If the guessed letter was correct, click on all the blanks where it occurs
  5. Click Done
  6. Computer will guess a letter... And so on
